Welcome to Musclewiki.


MuscleWiki is a fitness platform that provides an extensive library of exercises targeting specific muscle groups, making it a go-to resource for both beginners and advanced fitness enthusiasts. It allows users to explore exercises based on the muscle group they want to work on, such as arms, chest, legs, or back. The platform is free to use, making it a valuable resource for individuals who may not have access to a personal trainer or prefer to work out independently.
